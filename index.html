2. Decrypt it with your exteranl and save the decrypted plaintext file as temporary file (on external device).
3. "Browse" the temporary file , delete it and "Put" it and then
3.1. "View" it (when formed csv) to have it as a table or
3.2. "Mask" it to have its SHA-256 as its id.
3.2.1. Now you can cut and or paste the "Mask"ed over other files to produce your iiaom or any other id (for having such ids, by being in first column, indexing their reputation earned in your transactions in distributed table "View"ed from csv table being download/uploaded eg. to dropcanvas).
3.2.1.1. You make your iiaom by symmetrical encrypting your image, then "Put", "Mask" and cut it, then "Put" and "Mask the image and then paste and "Mask".

on the server, each fields is encrypted by the pub key of the scope defined by the column, where the scopes are:
0 of the data of owner of the record,
1 of data visible to the owner as one of many children of first ancestor etc.
